film in the gaps is your daily chance to link two films in as few steps as possible.

build a solution connecting films to cast & crew, to their filmographies, and so on.

each puzzle has a solution of six or fewer steps. if you need more context about a film or person click to see their details on TMDB.

e.g. Spider-Man: Far From Home starred Jake Gyllenhaal who played Jack Twist in Brokeback Mountain which was directed by Ang Lee who also directed Hulk which starred Jennifer Connelly who appeared in Spider-Man: Homecoming!
