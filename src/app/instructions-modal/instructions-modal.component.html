film in the gaps: link two films in as few steps as possible. each puzzle has a solution of six or fewer steps.

select a cast or crew member from the first film, then select a film from that person's filmography. repeat until you reach the last film.

e.g. Spider-Man: Far From Home stars Jake Gyllenhaal who plays Jack Twist in Brokeback Mountain which is directed by Ang Lee who also directs Hulk which stars Jennifer Connelly who appears in Spider-Man: Homecoming!
